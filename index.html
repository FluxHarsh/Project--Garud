About Team Garud

Team Garud is a robotics and aeromodelling StartUp at MANIT Bhopal. We work across mechanical design, electronics, coding, and prototyping to build robots and RC aircraft for competitions and workshops. Our team focuses on practical learning — designing CAD models, building circuits, writing embedded code, and testing systems on track or field. We also take part in tech events, robotics challenges, and conduct hands-on workshops in different colleges.
